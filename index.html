Roots & Vision

Hello! I am an undergraduate student at UC Davis pursuing a Bachelor of Science in International Agriculture Development (Expected June 2026). My academic journey is deeply rooted in understanding the botany and physiology of cultivated plants and the soils in our environment.

I have a strong passion for small-scale organic mixed vegetable production. As the owner of Plants4You, I have cultivated and propagated hundreds of drought-resistant and carnivorous plants, managing everything from sustainable pest control using natural predators like ladybugs, to running logistics and optimizing shipping inventories.

My goal in taking the Generative AI for Work & Research course is to merge traditional agricultural stewardship with modern no-code AI tools, automating administrative tasks so we can spend less time at the desk and more time in the soil.

Experience Highlights

  • Entrepreneurship: Managed an Etsy shop with over 500 sales, optimizing inventory systems in Excel.
  • Cultivation: Propagated 100+ plants from seedlings with meticulous structural pruning and watering.
  • Community Health: Volunteered at Knights Landing One Health Center, serving as a Tagalog interpreter and streamlining patient check-ins.
  • Skills: Bilingual in English & Tagalog, proficient in Microsoft Office, and currently expanding into Generative AI workflows.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort bootcamp hosted by The GRAPH Courses. It focuses on utilizing no-code AI tools for responsible task automation, empowering researchers and professionals to optimize their productivity.

Key Features

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Personalized feedback on assessments
  • Capstone project coaching
  • Responsible and ethical AI utilization

Core Tools Explored

  • ChatGPT Canvas, Code Interpreter & Replit
  • Julius & TheBricks for Data Analysis
  • Elicit & LiteRev for Literature Reviews
  • Botpress for Custom Chatbots & Agents
